Responsive Workspace

Progressive spoken-English platform for learners, teachers, and recruiters.

This frontend keeps all active flows in one responsive interface: onboarding, role dashboards, learner sessions, teacher class operations, and recruiter screening workflows with Business English support.

Practice EngineStart sessions, submit responses, and track progress with IELTS or CEFR mapping.
Teaching ConsoleCreate classes, inspect learner groups, and monitor delivery status.
Recruiter HubCreate screening packs and evaluate candidates with role-aligned criteria.
